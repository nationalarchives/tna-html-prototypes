Health And Social Services And Social Security Adjudications And Payments Act 1983

The Whole Act

Introductory Text

Part I Driving Licences

1. Abolition of special licences for driving HGVs and PSVs.

2. Special provisions relating to licensing of drivers of LGVs and PCVs.

3. Exceptions to prohibition on driving without a licence.

4. Tests of competence and related conditions of entitlement to driving licence.

5. Refusal or revocation of licences on grounds of physical unfitness.

5A. Compulsory training courses for riders of motor cycles.

6. Minor, consequential and further amendments.

Part II Driver Information Systems

Preliminary

7. Definitions of driver information systems etc.

Licensing for driver information systems

8. Requirement for licence to operate driver information system.

9. Operators licences.

9A. Sanctions for operating outside licence area or for breach of licence conditions.

Supplementary

10. Power to install apparatus etc.

11. Application of other Acts and protective provisions in relation to licence holders.

12. Offences by officers of bodies corporate.

13. Regulations and orders.

Part III General

14. Repeals.

15. Citation, commencement and extent.
