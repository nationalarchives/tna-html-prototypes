Finding funding

The most effective archives develop strong funding models to support secure, sustainable, resilient and innovative services. Here we provide information to help archive services develop fundraising strategies, identify appropriate funding sources and explore a range of fundraising techniques.

Discover resources from the former Fundraising for Archives programme.

Archives Revealed

Archives Revealed is a partnership programme between The National Archives and The Pilgrim Trust, and is the only funding stream in the UK dedicated to cataloguing and unlocking archives.

How to fundraise

These pages are designed to support people within the sector who are working to raise funds either for a project, a capital appeal or core costs.

Further help and support

We can provide advice and support to help you apply to either of our cataloguing or scoping grant funding strands.

Funding partners

Archives Revealed is a partnership programme between The National Archives and The Pilgrim Trust, additionally funded by The Wolfson Foundation.
